Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to join BUAME 2R? expand_more
Yes, registering as a provider is completely free. We may charge a small commission on completed jobs or successful sales, depending on your category.
What documents do I need for verification? expand_more
You will need a valid Ghana Card. Drivers will also need a valid driver's license. Traders may need business registration documents for higher tiers.
How do I get paid? expand_more
Payments are secured through Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo). You can withdraw your earnings directly to your wallet instantly.
