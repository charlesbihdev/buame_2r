Frequently Asked Questions

How do I identify a verified artisan? keyboard_arrow_down
Look for the green shield icon next to their profile name. You can also tap on their profile to see their "Member Since" date and read reviews from other neighbors in Sefwi Bekwai.
What should I do if a driver asks for offline payment? keyboard_arrow_down
We strongly recommend keeping all payments within the BUAME 2R platform to ensure you are covered by our safety guarantee. If a driver insists on offline payment, please decline and report them using the "Report Issue" button immediately.
How do I report a suspicious job posting? keyboard_arrow_down
On every job listing page, there is a "Report Job" flag icon located near the bottom description. Click it and select "Suspicious or Scam" to alert our safety team. We review all reports within 24 hours.

Need Immediate Help?

If you are in an emergency situation involving personal safety, please contact the local authorities immediately.
